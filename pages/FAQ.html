GUIDE & SUPPORT

GENERAL INFO

Palworld is currently available on PC (Steam & Microsoft Store), Xbox Series X|S, and Xbox One.

Xbox & PC Game Pass: Yes, crossplay works perfectly.
Steam & Xbox: Not currently supported, but the developers plan to add it in a future update.

PvP is not available at launch (Early Access). However, an Arena mode for Pal battles is confirmed for future updates.
